Welcome to my personal technology blog.

This space is where I document my hands-on learning journey in **DevOps, cloud technologies, automation, and other software engineering topics**. Here, I share my **labs, experiments, troubleshooting experiences, and solutions** that I encounter while learning and building projects.

The goal of this blog is to:
- Record practical knowledge from real labs and projects
- Document challenges I face and the solutions I discover
- Share useful insights and step-by-step troubleshooting with others learning similar technologies
- Build a portfolio that reflects my technical growth

## What You Will Find Here

Most content will focus on **DevOps practices and infrastructure technologies**, including:

- Linux system administration and troubleshooting
- Cloud platforms (AWS, etc.) and hands-on labs
- Docker and container orchestration
- CI/CD pipelines and automation
- Monitoring, logging, and troubleshooting techniques
- Python scripting for automation
- Infrastructure issues and resolutions

Whenever I encounter a problem in my labs or projects, I try to **document the troubleshooting steps and the solution**, so others can benefit from my experience.

## Why This Blog Exists

Technology evolves quickly, and learning by doing is essential. By documenting my **labs, problems, and solutions**, I aim to:

- Keep a personal knowledge base
- Improve my problem-solving skills
- Provide helpful references for others
- Showcase my practical DevOps experience
